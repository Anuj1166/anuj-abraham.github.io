About

I am an experienced researcher in Control Engineering, Artificial Intelligence, Computer Vision, and Smart Mobility. My work focuses on intelligent transportation systems, autonomous vehicles, AI-driven perception, and real-time deployment. This encompasses the use of modeling and control to solve science and engineering problems.

I am currently a Research Associate at IIT Goa, working on AI-driven marine species identification and smart mobility systems.

My research interests are in the area of systems & control with a strong background in the modeling of complex systems, optimization, & control of a large-scale system with applications to intelligent transportation systems, autonomous systems, and process control & instrumentation. I have several years of expertise in the field of control engineering and smart mobility. I am skilled in traffic simulation, control theory, C, C++, Robot Operating System (ROS), MATLAB, VISSIM, and VTD.

I earned my Ph.D. in the faculty of Electrical engineering at Anna University, Chennai-India in February 2017. Moreover, I was a Postdoctoral fellow at Energy Research Institute@NTU (ERIAN) and Smart Mobility Experience Lab (SMEL) of the School of Electrical and Electronic Engineering at the Nanyang Technological University (NTU) in Singapore (2017–2019). I am a recipient of the Anna Centenary Research Fellowship (ACRF) for 3 years (2012-2015) and a scholarship for an exchange Ph.D. - 6 months mobility period at University of Pardubice, Czech Republic through Erasmus Mundus Action 2 (EMA2) Svaagata.eu project (2014-2015). I have teaching experience of more than 5 years as Assistant Professor in the Department of Applied Electronics & Instrumentation at the Rajagiri School of Engineering and Technology (RSET), APJ Abdul Kalam Technological University (KTU) in Kerala- India. Additonally, served as Guest Lecturer at Anna University, Chennai-India for 1.5 years.
